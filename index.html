About Me

I am a recent graduate of General Assembly Software Engineering Immersive Program. I am passionate about bringing equality to the tech field and world in general. I am committed to my values and you will rarely find me in my comfort zone.

My interest in programming started with my sister who graduated with a Computer Science degree. It took some time for me to realize I had what it takes to work in the tech field and a lot of it had to do with meeting amazing leading women of the Chicago tech community who inspired me further to start my career in tech.

Throughout my journey I learned that you cannot grow and learn without experiencing failure, rejection and self-doubt. That is why I mastered the skill of getting back on my feet very fast and keep on going.
